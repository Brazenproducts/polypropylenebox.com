PP Bulk Containers vs Cardboard Gaylords

Cardboard gaylords are single-use and crush under moisture. PP bulk containers are built for repeat use in tough industrial environments:

  • Waterproof walls — no collapse in wet/cold environments
  • Forklift pockets — 4-way entry standard
  • Reusable 200–500 trips — dramatic cost reduction vs cardboard
