Why PP for Produce?

Fresh produce distribution demands packaging that can handle humidity, cold temps, pressure washing, and multiple cycles — cardboard does none of those things well. PP corrugated does all of them:

  • Vented walls promote airflow to reduce spoilage
  • 100% waterproof — stands up to ice, condensation, hosing
  • USDA accepted for food contact use
  • FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 compliant
  • No mold, no bacteria harborage between cycles
